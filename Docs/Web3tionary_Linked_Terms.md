# Web3tionary-Linked Terms  
*How Microscopic Gold concepts connect to the Web3tionary.*

This document identifies the terms, concepts, and coined words originating from the **Microscopic Gold ecosystem** that are intended for inclusion in the **Web3tionary**, along with reasoning notes and future placement references.

Its purpose is to:

- maintain clarity across repos  
- prevent duplication  
- preserve the origin relationships of new terminology  
- document why certain terms belong in the cultural dictionary of Web3  

---

## 1. Core Terms Originating From Microscopic Gold

These terms directly represent Microscopic Gold and its sub-brands, and therefore belong in the Web3tionary as *foundational cultural terms*.

### **1.1 Microscopic Gold**
**Type:** Concept-level entry  
**Status:** Pending  
**Why it belongs:**  
Represents a philosophical shift in how Web3 interprets fractional value, micro-ownership, and symbolic wealth.  
Will anchor a future category inside the Web3tionary: **Micro-Value Culture**.

---

### **1.2 paxgdrops**
**Type:** Functional/Concept  
**Status:** Pending  
**Why it belongs:**  
Extends the “Random Rain” lineage into a gold-backed conceptual distribution model.  
Shows how micro-value can flow in symbolic, educational, or gratitude-based ways.

---

### **1.3 modernminers**
**Type:** Identity Term  
**Status:** Pending  
**Why it belongs:**  
Defines a new type of “miner”: one who mines information rather than earth.  
Culturally relevant for the future of Web3 education and micro-value literacy.

---

### **1.4 digitalpaydirt**
**Type:** Concept + Metaphor  
**Status:** Pending  
**Why it belongs:**  
Reframes the historical idea of paydirt into a transparent, digital, discovery-based experience.  
A strong example of how metaphors translate into Web3 cultural language.

---

### **1.5 golddustcoins**
**Type:** Emotional/Experiential Term  
**Status:** Pending  
**Why it belongs:**  
Captures the symbolic weight of micro-ownership (“dust-sized value that still matters”).  
Important for defining emotional value in a fractional world.

---

### **1.6 pennygold**
**Type:** Micro-savings Term  
**Status:** Pending  
**Why it belongs:**  
An entry that ties old forms of slow accumulation (jars of pennies) to modern micro-ownership of asset-backed value.
